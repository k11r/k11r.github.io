A Solo feature by Mün Lün Kir Sa.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
2003
You've just come in from recess, you are walking with
your class to the computer lab. You arrive and take
a seat. The room is sweaty and warm, your energy is
spent. In a content exhalation you wake your white plastic
dell tower with a wave of the mouse. Pre-logged-in Windows
XP flashes to life. You excitedly navigate the home menu searching
for one thing. Craig Hickman's KidPix.
